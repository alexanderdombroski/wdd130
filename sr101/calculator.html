Instructions

  • Select which deck you are using.
  • Select a faction if you would like to narrow your search.
  • Type in cards to see how strong they are.
  • A drop down menu can also display a list of available cards.
  • If you would like to scrap a card, just remove it from the menu.
  • Change other settings if needed.
  • Besides size and cycling, averages are measured by amount per turn

Key

  • Size: Deck size
  • Cycling: Turns required to draw your whole deck
  • Value: Average total cost of cards (per turn)
  • Discard: Cards you force the opponent to discard
  • Scrap: Cards you can scrap
  • T-Scrap: Cards you can scrap in the trade row
  • Defence: Average defence per turn

Disclaimers

  • These stats best represent a situation where you draw a new hand from a shuffled deck.
  • These stats do not fully take into account drawing a card that let's you draw another card. A deck with majority of cards letting you draw a card may not be perfectly represented.
  • This calculator may not be updated with new cards coming out.
  • Due to the way data validation and drop downs works in google sheets, some cards will throw an incorrect error.
  • You'll need to give the app permissions if you want to use the reset script button
  • This is most easily done in a new tab.
